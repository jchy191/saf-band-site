CAREER

Joining the ranks of the nation's musical elite, a career as a military musician encompasses being ambassadors of the nation in a organization at the front line of diplomacy. Our military musicians are deployed for parades and ceremonies to add pomp and pageantry. Expect deployments that involve performing for the President and other foreign dignitaries in the welcome functions and guard of honor ceremonies.
