OUR PEOPLE

ACTING DIRECTOR OF MUSIC

ME4 Ong Wee Hong

SAF CEREMONIAL BAND B

ME4 Ong started his music career as a piano teacher and a freelance lounge pianist. In 1990, he joined the Singapore Infantry Regiment (SIR) Band as a clarinetist, and obtained the Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music (ABRSM) Grade 8 certification within 3 years.

In 1992, ME4 Ong was selected to attend the Assistant Band Leader Course at the Defence Force School of Music in Australia where he studied conducting and music arrangement. He was appointed as the Assistant Band Sergeant Major on his return and was actively involved in arranging music for wind band, combo band, and chamber ensembles. During the merger of the tri-service bands in 1994 and the SAF (Singapore Armed Forces) Bandâ€™s transformation, ME4 Ong also helped to formalise new administrative and operating procedures in the bands.

Throughout his service, he has held several key appointments in the SAF Band. He has previously been appointed Concertmaster, Woodwind Group Leader, Section Leader, and Combo IC. He currently serves as the Acting Director of Music of SAF Ceremonial Band B.
